THE ESSENTIALS

Three things every plant asks for

☀️

Light

Most houseplants love bright, indirect light. A spot near a window that never gets harsh noon sun is the sweet spot.

💧

Water

Check the top inch of soil. Dry? Water deeply until it drains. Still damp? Wait. Overwatering is the #1 mistake.

🌡️

Warmth

Keep them between 18–24°C and away from cold drafts. Steady beats dramatic.
